## 2025-05-14 - [Contact Form Feedback and Accessibility]
**Learning:** Forms without feedback states (loading/success) can lead to user confusion and duplicate submissions. Ensuring proper ARIA associations (htmlFor/id) and HTML5 validation (required) are baseline requirements for a professional UX.
**Action:** Always implement 'isSending' and 'isSent' states for async form submissions and verify label-input associations.
